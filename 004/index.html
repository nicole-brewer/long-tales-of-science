Trial by Fire - Episode 3

Posted on Wednesday, May 18, 2022 | computational psychology, testing, user interface, graduate student, management
In this episode, Nicole interviews Elizabeth Aslinger, who recently defended her dissertation for which she used mathematical modeling to identify people who have schizophrenia. We talk about her experience managing software and students in a computational psychology lab, teaching herself standards of practice, and what her priorities as she is choosing the next step in her career.

Her substantive projects have involved investigations of affective and interpersonal processes, narcissism, externalizing symptoms (e.g., alcohol use, hostility), PTSD, suicidality, cognitive neuroscience (in particular, learning and decision-making), and psychosis.
